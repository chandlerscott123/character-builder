Character Name

High Elf

High Elves are known as the intellectuals of the world.
They are very pretentious, often peering down at village folk
from their spired walls. This weakness is their strength, for
their mental fortitude grants them gifts in charisma, intelligence,
and alchemy.

Dark Elf

The fallen angels of the High Elves. Due to their deftness and
stealth, some say that Dark Elves are descendants of the night itself.
Gifted in theivery, magic,
and alchemy.

Paladin

Only a fool would challenge the Paladin in hand to hand combat!
The Paladins brutish strength makes up most of their power,
so their capacity to reason through a situation is very limited. That said,there is a reason the Paladins are royalty in the kingdoms of the world. Gifted in strength, armor,
and blacksmithing.

Alethi Lighteyes

The rulers of the Vorin kingdoms. These are humanoids of Herald-chosen
royalty. Although some Lighteyes fight bravely in battle, the most
accurate words to describe a light eyes would be noble, pretentious
and sheltered. Gifted in divinity, charisma,
and bartering.
